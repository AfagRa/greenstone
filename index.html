Our Product

Self-healing hydrogel capsules

Our innovative product harnesses the power of biology to create construction materials that heal themselves. We encapsulate beneficial bacteria within hydrogel capsules that are mixed into concrete at the point of casting.

When cracks form and water enters, the capsules rupture and release bacteria that precipitate calcium carbonate—effectively sealing the crack and restoring structural integrity. This approach reduces long-term maintenance, extends building lifespan, and supports a more sustainable built environment.

Technology

Bacteria Cultivation

We cultivate specific strains of bacteria that produce calcium carbonate when activated by moisture, forming the basis of our self-healing mechanism.

Hydrogel Preparation

Bacteria are encapsulated in a durable hydrogel matrix that protects them until cracks allow water to trigger the healing reaction.

Capsules Ready for Cement

The finished capsules are designed to be mixed directly into cement, ensuring even distribution and long-term stability within the structure.

About Us

Green Stone is a biotech company focused on sustainable construction. Our mission is to bring nature's self-healing capabilities to the built environment, reducing waste and extending the life of infrastructure.

We combine microbiology, materials science, and engineering to develop solutions that make concrete more durable and eco-friendly. Based in Baku, Azerbaijan, we work with partners globally to build a more resilient future.

We were proud to participate in the Hello Baku ID event, where we showcased our self-healing concrete technology to investors and industry leaders. The event provided a platform to connect with forward-thinking partners who share our vision for sustainable infrastructure.

Recognition at events like Baku ID reinforces our commitment to innovation and helps us scale our impact in the region and beyond.
